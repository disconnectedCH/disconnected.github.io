We want our objectives to have a direct impact on our users, so the "My progress" section will record your evolution along this path of good habits.

How does "My progress" work?

My progress is the area that allows you to measure your mobile usage regulation based on the time you use your phone, represented in a weekly graph where you will see your daily usage time per day.

Additionally, you will be able to see the time you spend on: social networks, productivity and health or recreation time.

The more you keep up your streak of staying within the 3 hour per day maximum usage limit, the more your little plant will be able to grow with you.
