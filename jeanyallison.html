How Disconnected Works?

In terms of usability, the tools offered by disconnected are based on time accounting so that users can focus on their activities without digital interventions.


But Disconnected is also designed to help the user on a personal level, providing solutions for recreational activities to initiate self-discovery and leave aside technological dependence without being boring.

Disconnected Features

Focus Time

It allows you to concentrate on your work, finish your work efficiently and say goodbye to procrastination.

Disconnected

Disconnected is an application blocker, a website blocker, and a notification blocker... all in one! What do you want to block?

Recomendations

It is a list of activities, which will help you kill boredom and start focusing on what really matters, spending time with yourself.

A little partner

Disconnected gives you a little seedling that together will grow little by little as your organization and time management become better.

Statistics

It takes care of analyzing work time and task completion, time spent working each day/week/month and time ratio of your projects. Make time traceable.
